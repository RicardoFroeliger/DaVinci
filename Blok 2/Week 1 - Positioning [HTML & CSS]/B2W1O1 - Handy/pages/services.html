Services

DaVinci Handy has a great history and over time many new construction projects.
You can think of a detached house, semi-detached houses or even a block of houses.
We are happy to look for the most ideal home with you, perfectly fitting within your budget in combination with the best construction quality and the right advice from A to Z. So are you walking around with an idea to start building yourself? Then come to DaVinci Handy and get to know our personal way of working.

Placing a contruction or superstructure is one of the regular activities at DaVinci Handy. We have already been able to provide many customers with an efficient extension of their home. Thanks to our extensive experience, our own professionals and reliable partners, we can perfectly realize any assignment. From the very first moment we like to think along with you and provide you with the most reliable advice. Always taking into account the technical feasibility, the budget and regulations regarding building in the district and/or municipality.
